Scott Mason - Data Scientist - Architect - Director


Scott has over has over 15 years of experience designing, developing, and building, dynamic teams that have to come together to a consensus to accomplish agreed upon goals for small, medium, Fortune 500 companies.

I helped launch a start-up from zero to a $100 million in sales in its first year of operations at Hyundai Capital Insurance Services.

  •   -  Managed an eCommerce Supply Chain system carrying $11 Billion in sales
  •   -  Managed small teams of 6 to 12 people
  •   -  Managed large teams of 212, 106, and 108 people

Scott has been responsible for managing personnel, recruiting and selecting them, training them, conducting performance assessments, work assignments, gathering metrics for better planning, and managing budgets in excess of $72 million. Scott has acted as the primary customer contact for project activities as well as leading project review sessions with customers and vendors to discuss cost, schedule, and performance.

Scott has an innovative approach to solving problems; He brings unique solutions, functionality, reliability, manageability, and performance as major benefits to management as well as customers.

  •   -  Scott has a Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration from the University of Oregon
  •   -  My experience above demonstrates a self-starter approach with the ability to multi-task
  •   -  Experience with Nike, Oakely, Addias, and others gives me a unique perspective in working some of the best run innovators in the world.
  •   -  Scott has direct experience in Fortune 500 companies financial systems.
  •   -  Scott has strong management experience and organizational skills in planning, delegating, and program development.
  •   -  Scott has strong written and oral communication skills, with an ability to convey a vision of the organization’s strategic future to all stakeholders.

Scott started his career:
  •   -  As a DBA
  •   -  Then progressed to a Data Architect

  •   -  Then began to develop the Front End with HTLM, Javascript, Angular, Next.JS, and frameworks

Scott continues stay involved as a Solution Architect.
